How to capture muted videos 🤫

Your browser will pop up a sharing dialog. Two things matter:

  1. Choose Chrome Tab (not Window or Screen) and pick the tab playing your video.
  2. Tick the “Also share tab audio” checkbox at the bottom of the dialog.

Audio is captured at the source, so you can mute your speakers and the agent still hears everything. The video can play silently for you while NoteAgent listens. 🪄

Works in Chrome, Edge, Opera. Firefox/Safari have limited tab-audio support.
